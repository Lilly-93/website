CULVERT



Created by
Media - Lilly Parr
Sound - Tom Crosley Thorne

‘Culvert’ is a 360 degree virtual reality video that follows the natural path of the River Frome as it winds under the city of Bristol. Placing you into various locations along its original route, we invite you to step into a juxtaposed environment where the river has taken back its original course. You will see the city in a way you have never seen before, and hear how the city of Bristol could have been if we had let it rest. We invite you to ask questions about your connection to the hidden river Frome, our manipulation of nature and how we can respond to changing climates.
